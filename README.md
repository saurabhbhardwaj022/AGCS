AGCS – AVPL Ground Control Station

AGCS (AVPL Ground Control Station) is a powerful, reliable, and user-friendly ground control application developed for AVPL International and its clients. Designed to support professional UAV operations, AGCS provides complete flight control, mission planning, and vehicle configuration through a modern and intuitive interface.

AGCS is built to serve both first-time operators and experienced drone professionals. The application simplifies complex UAV operations while maintaining high levels of precision, safety, and operational control. Its streamlined workflow ensures faster mission preparation and efficient in-flight monitoring.

Key Capabilities

Full flight control and real-time telemetry monitoring

Advanced mission planning with waypoint-based navigation

Support for MAVLink-enabled UAV platforms

Vehicle setup and configuration for PX4 and ArduPilot-based drones

Pre-flight checks, parameter tuning, and calibration tools

Live map view, flight status indicators, and mission progress tracking

Designed for industrial, enterprise, and professional drone use cases

AGCS is tailored to meet AVPL operational standards and client requirements, ensuring dependable performance across surveying, mapping, inspection, training, and commercial drone missions.
